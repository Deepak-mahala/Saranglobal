About Us

Saran Global Enterprises is one of the leading merchant exporters in India. We specialize in exporting and importing various Stationary item, Agricultural Commodities, Grains & Pulses, Spices, Rice, Sugar, FMCG, Frozen Food, Oils, Flour items, Premix, Grocery & Plastic Products, Engineering Items &Machineries, Cloths, Footwear, Fire & Safety Items, Building Materials, Cattle Feeds & General Products from India as well as all over the world.

We are a professional Merchant Exporter, with focus on quality, safety & timely delivery. Our exposure & expertise of over one decades, in serving the export market helps us to understand the business, with umpteen focus.

We are a team of passionate professionals, with a hands-on approach towards business. Our endeavor is to keep up to the promise & deliver satisfaction.

Our wide network has spread its wings covering the markets of Africa, Asia, Middle East, Europe and many more.
